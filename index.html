Jaagat Prashar

I'm a sophomore studying CS/Math at Stanford. I grew up in India, Azerbaijan, Dubai, and Texas.

I value human connection, which has inspired a lot of my work across deep learning, autonomy, public policy, venture capital, and healthcare. I enjoy animating the inanimate (Gödel Escher Bach).

Previously: General Motors/Cruise (ADAS Team), where I trained VLAs for self-driving, deep learning for drug discovery (ACM, ISEF). Currently, I'm researching how to improve the abilities of robotic systems to perceive, reason, and act using VLAs.

I co-host the STARTS (STEM+Arts) podcast, where I challenge the conventional norm through thought-provoking conversations. I am also fortunate enough to be a VC fellow at NFX. In my free-time, I can be seen relishing a cup of coffee while coding or reading, playing with my Labrador-Saluki-Akita Buster, and at the gym.
